**Crosschain Live** is an interoperability-focused livestream and podcast where we bring together builders, founders, and community members from across Web3. This weekly livestream is hosted by [Kanishk Khurana](https://x.com/kanishkkhurana), Senior DevRel at Across.

The show is equal parts:

- Crosschain and interoperability deep dives
- Project spotlights and unapologetic shilling
- Candid conversations, hot takes, and spicy topics
- Community, culture, and the realities of building in crypto, specially from a developer's standpoint

This is not a polished PR interview, it's a relaxed, open conversation where guests can speak freely, share updates, and explore ideas that don't always fit into formal panels.

---

## Who Should Apply?

You should apply if you are:

- A founder or core contributor to a crypto project
- Building or experimenting with crosschain / interop tech
- A community lead, researcher, or ecosystem contributor
- Someone with strong opinions and interesting perspectives
- Excited to talk openly and engage with a live audience

Early-stage projects and first-time speakers are welcome.
